Project Team

The Project is mainly conducted by Fengdi Huang(231AHG003) and Zhen Pan(231AHG002) in the course of Introduction to Digital Humanities.

Both of us major in Digital Humanities.

The project is completed with python web-scraping, a bunch of NLP tools and the assistance of ChatGpt and Gemini.

Vision

Some of you might not know freedom is a luxury in many parts of the world including China.

The long-term vision of the project is to get the hang of how the chinese government's political spectrum diverges from the general public's opinions. If possible, we target to pinpoint of the differences and contribute to the freedom of speech in China at least.

This project might serve as the preliminary topic of our further master's thesis. Hopefully, we could train our own model and use more robust statictical approaches such as cosine similarity and KL Divergence to obtain in-depth findings so that we could really make a difference in the near future.
